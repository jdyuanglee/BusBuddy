Welcome to peace of mind

With the BusBuddy multi-stage process, you should be comfortable knowing your child is making it to school safely.

Stage 1: Driver Starts the route

Each morning, the bus driver will start the route and let each connected parent know the bus is on its way.

Stage 2: Child scans on

When the bus arrives, your child scans their card onto our custom built Buddy on board. An SMS message will be sent to each connected parent.

Stage 3: Driver arrives at school

When the driver gets to school, another SMS notification will be sent to the parent knowing the child made it to school safely that day.
